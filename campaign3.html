Save the Evidence

It’s tempting to delete hurtful messages right away, but don’t do it just yet. Save the evidence — take screenshots and back up the messages. This proof can help you when reporting the situation or if things get serious. Cyberbullying often hides behind screens, but saving the facts brings the truth to light. Keep a record. It might feel small, but it could make a huge difference later.
