talk to Someone

No one should suffer in silence. If you’re feeling sad, anxious, or unsafe because of something happening online, talk to someone you trust. Whether it’s a parent, teacher, school counselor, or a close friend — there is always someone who cares and wants to help. Speaking up isn’t weak, it’s brave. You’re never alone in this. The first step to healing is sharing what you’re going through.
