Dont' Respond

When someone tries to hurt you online, your best weapon can be silence. Responding to hateful messages often makes things worse — it gives bullies exactly what they want: your attention. By choosing not to reply, you're showing maturity and strength. You’re saying, “I’m better than this.” Your peace is more powerful than any insult. Walk away, breathe, and remember: not every message deserves your energy.
