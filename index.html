What is cyberbullying

cyper bulling is when someone uses digital devices like phones, computers, or social media platforms to harm, threaten, or embarrass anothe person it can include mean messages, spreading lies, sharing private photos without permission or excluding someone online.

Harmful Messages

Sending mean or threatening messages repeatedly to someone online.

Spreading Rumors

Posting false information or lies about someone to damage their reputation.

Exclusion

Deliberately excluding someone from an online group or activity.
